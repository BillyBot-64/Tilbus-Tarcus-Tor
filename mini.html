PLAY THE MINI TILBUS TARCUS TOR GAME

Play original version here

Strategically place your own pieces on any square that is not occupied twice. Get in 3 in a row to win. If you win first, you get points based on how many rows you scored on your turn. The game is over once all squares become occupied. If multiple people get the same final score, then they tie

This is a 3 player game.
The order goes: Sad face -> Hammer and Sickle -> Triangle

If you see the & symbol, then those two players both drew.
